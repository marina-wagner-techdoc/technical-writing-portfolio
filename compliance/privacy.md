# Privacy & Data Protection (fitElle)

## Overview

The fitElle app focuses on privacy, anonymization, and secure handling of user data.

---

## GDPR Considerations

- Collection of personal health data requires explicit consent  
- Clear communication of data usage  

---

## Example – Privacy Notice

### Before
We use your data to improve the app.

### After
We use your data to provide personalized recommendations and improve your experience.  
Your data is processed securely and is not shared with third parties without your consent.

✅ transparent  
✅ user-focused  
✅ GDPR-aligned wording  

---

## UX & Privacy

- Clear explanations of what data is collected  
- Avoid technical jargon  
- Build user trust through transparency
